I feel like the theme to Dawson's Creek should be playing. Or maybe Whatcha Say 
Today is a sad day (technically the same day as the Woodchuck debacle, but I wanted two posts). The 10lb bag of Krusteaz instant pancake mix that we got for Tom's birthday over a year ago has finally expired. It made it through some chocolate chip bacon beer pancakes on Tom's birthday, a bunch of normal pancakes inbetween, and saw the birth of my experimentation with beer pancakes in general.

In the interim, it'll be replaced by whatever pancake supplies I have handy, but ultimately will likely be replaced by another 10lb CostCo bag, because it's way cheaper/easier to make beer pancakes with pancake mix than to do the whole bisquick/milk/egg thing.
I'm lookin' at you, Roundy's All-Purpose Baking Mix
Keep an eye out for the successor in future posts. In the meantime, shed a tear for our fallen bretheren, and go make yourself some pancakes.
